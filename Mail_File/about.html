Who We Are

The Story Behind
Jexira

Founded in 2005 in Jamnagar, Gujarat — the brass capital of India — Jexira Bath Products has grown from a small workshop into one of India's most trusted manufacturers of premium bathroom accessories and stainless steel fittings.

"We don't just manufacture products — we craft experiences that transform bathrooms into spaces of elegance and function."

With a state-of-the-art production facility, a dedicated R&D team, and a commitment to zero-compromise quality, we serve dealers, distributors, builders, and interior designers across pan India.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Growth

From a small Jamnagar workshop to a pan-India brand — our story is built on passion and precision.

2005

The Beginning

Jexira was founded in Jamnagar, Gujarat with a small team of 12 craftsmen and a vision to make premium bath fittings accessible to all.

2010

Factory Expansion

Expanded to a 20,000 sq ft state-of-the-art manufacturing facility with CNC machines and a dedicated quality lab.

2014

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification — cementing our commitment to international quality standards in every product.

2018

Pan India Network

Established a dealer & distributor network spanning 28 states with 1,000+ channel partners across India.

2022

PVD Coating Line

Launched advanced PVD gold, black, and rose-gold coating technology — expanding into premium designer segment.

2026

Today & Beyond

50,000+ happy clients, 500+ SKUs, and a growing export footprint. We continue to set new benchmarks in quality and innovation.
